Assessment Generator

The Assessment Generator is a powerful toolset designed to streamline the creation and management of question papers. It interfaces with a question bank to offer advanced functionalities, including subset selection, versioning, and question allocation. Whether you’re designing a single exam or multiple versions, this tool simplifies the entire process while maintaining consistency and quality.

Features

Discover powerful tools to simplify assessment creation, enhance question management, and ensure flawless consistency across multiple exam versions with unmatched efficiency.

Seamless Integration

Works effortlessly with a question bank for streamlined operations.

Flexible Subset Selection

Easily select specific questions to tailor assessments for various needs

Versioning Support

Create multiple versions or sets of a question paper with consistency.

Multi-Set Allocation

Generate and allocate varied question paper sets for fairness and customization.

Difficulty Level Adjustment

Categorize and select questions based on difficulty levels for balanced assessments.

Randomized Question Order

Shuffle questions to ensure unique papers for each version.

Auto-Generated Reports

Get insights on the distribution and content of each assessment set.

Customizable Formatting

Design question papers with personalized headers, footers, and layouts.

Why Choose Assessment Generator Tool?

As an educator, creating fair and comprehensive question papers can be time-consuming. Assessment Generator is your open-source, customizable tool that lets you create high-quality question banks quickly and easily—saving you time to focus on what matters most: teaching.
