Empowering Education for All

EduReach is an Open-Source Educational Technologies platform, where we provide versatile and customizable Learning Management System (LMS) tools for digital education.

This initiative is aimed at lowering the barrier to entry for digital education adoption in underprivileged learning environments which can lead to revolutionary changes in the quality of education that a child receives.

BACKGROUND

Enhancing Education

We believe quality education should be accessible to all. Our platform empowers underfunded learning environments with essential tools, enhancing access to digital content, assessments, and insights.

Adapting Education to Every Need

The platform ensures that the most suitable tools for digital education are freely available and can be tailored to meet the diverse needs of different learning environments.

Education For All

In alignment with the UN Sustainable Development Goal 4: Quality Education for All, we are breaking barriers, bridging gaps, and aiming to bring transformative change to millions of lives.

About us

The EduReach project is an initiative at IIT Madras, carried out in the Wadhwani School of Data Science and AI. It is primarily funded through CSR contributions.
